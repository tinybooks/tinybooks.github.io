Speaking and Workshops

I’m a passionate speaker and have spoken at events, large and small, all over the world. I’ve delivered keynotes, opened and closed conferences, and run workshops on a wide variety of topics. I’m known for my entertaining style, which is educational, but – equally importantly – enjoyable.

In addition to speaking at Build, Smashing Conference, Industry and numerous other conferences, I’ve also worked with private clients, including Booking.com and the BBC, to deliver bespoke presentations and workshops.

I have over two decades of speaking experience. I’m able to customise my content to suit your audience, be it large or small, and I’d be happy to explore potential topics with you. I’ve provided a comprehensive list of my speaking and workshops below.

If you’re running a conference, I’d love to work with you. Get in touch.

Tried and Tested Knowledge

Murphy draws the content for Tiny Books from over a decade of working with creative businesses of all sizes. His material is tried and tested, through a combination of: consultancy, working with businesses in the creative industries; education, at The Belfast School of Art; and, finally, through material he delivered as a lead trainer on a number of creative startup programmes pioneered by the UK innovation charity, Nesta.

Through this work he has mentored a generation of designers, helping them develop the critical business skills required to succeed and thrive.
