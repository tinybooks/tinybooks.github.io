What's in the book?

  1. Introduction

    Why has there never been a better time to start? What about now is different? Here are concrete facts – about enablers that change the way we can do business now, powered by the web – that underpin my belief that now is the time to start.

  2. Chapter 1 · It Starts With You

    Find your passion. Find the spark you can use to start. Put everything behind your passion and turn it into a reality. Look deep inside and identify your inner purpose. Do that and you’ll have a stronger foundation on which to build a product with meaning.

  3. Chapter 2 · What’s your story?

    It’s important to identify your core values and, by extension, your story. Business is changing in this new century (and new millennium), and even with just fifteen years’ perspective, new ways are emerging to build businesses that are purpose-driven and built on core values.

  4. Chapter 3 · Designing Your Future

    Steal like an artist. Seek inspiration from others and gather just enough influences to give you a clear sense of direction. Define a goal using envisioning techniques, and map a path towards that goal, breaking it down into distinct deliverables.

  5. Case Studies

    Start! features three case studies you can draw inspiration from: Hiut Denim; Kano; and Sugru. All have lessons we can learn from: about the power of focus; about the importance of stories; and about the importance of persistence, and never giving up.

  6. Chapter 7 · Turn Your Idea Into a Reality

    In this final chapter I tie together everything that’s covered in the book. It’s about the importance of execution and how – although initially daunting – any complex task can be broken down into discrete and manageable elements.
